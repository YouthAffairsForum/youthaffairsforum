    • Prisha Ahuja (Founder)

    Raiyan Sadi Aditya (Co-Founder)


    Since you’re reading this, I assume you’re already familiar with YAF and its values, so I technically don't even have to introduce myself. YAF is an org that resonates with pretty much everything I stand for. From international relations to youth empowerment, from geopolitics to raw and uncensored journalism, when I look at YAF, it feels like looking at a mirror. And just like I want to make the reflection in the mirror look prettier, I want to make YAF a pioneering organization in youth empowerment and get more people to care about what truly matters. I want to give the youth a platform, a platform to channel their voices to the world stage, and a platform for them to discover the best in themselves. The serious stuff aside, I like to brew coffee, play the guitar, and listen to cigarettes after sex. I also read books when I have nothing better to do, mostly fantasy and gore, but I also read political stuff when I'm in that phase. Anyway, this is probably already longer than it should’ve been. I’ll stop. Reach out to me at adityaraiyansadi@gmail.com or @iloveglosss for any further inquiries.

    Govind Kheptal (Co-Founder)


    As Toni Morrison said, “if there is a book that you want to read but has not been written yet, then you must write it.” Thus, if there are initiatives we want to see being undertaken, we must create opportunities for them. As part of the Youth Affairs Forum, I want to make MUNs, debates, compelling discussions, nuanced content and unfiltered discourses on current affairs and much more accessible for present and future students, because those are all things that have driven me and continue to drive me through my youth. YAF feels like a diaristic celebration of all my passions, because that is the epicentre of all our efforts, as well as a communal ether where all of these memorable and amazing events can become ours. Speaking of sharing interests, I love reading books, sometimes writing them, moot courts, listening to music, playing the piano, and basically just yapping and consuming media. I am so grateful to have all of you as part of the YAF community, and you can find me at @lost.in.some.fantasy on instagram and at govindkhetpal14@gmail.com on email.
